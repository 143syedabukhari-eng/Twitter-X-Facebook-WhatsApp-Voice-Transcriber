Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the WhatsApp Voice Transcriber ("the Extension") handles your information. Your privacy is our top priority, and we are committed to being transparent about how your data is processed.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Extension is designed as a productivity tool for WhatsApp Web. It does not collect or store your personal data on any external servers owned by the developer.

  • API Keys: To function, the Extension requires an API key from a third-party provider (AssemblyAI, OpenAI, or Deepgram). These keys are stored locally in your browser’s chrome.storage.sync and are only used to authenticate your requests to these services.
  • Audio Content: When you click the "Transcribe" button, the Extension fetches the specific audio message from WhatsApp Web. This audio is sent directly to your chosen AI provider for processing. The Extension does not save or archive your audio files.
  • Transcripts: The resulting text is displayed only within your WhatsApp Web chat interface. We do not store or track the content of your messages.

2. Third-Party Services

To provide high-accuracy transcription and translation, the Extension interacts with the following third-party services. By using the Extension, you agree to the privacy policies of these providers:

  • Transcription Providers: Depending on your settings, audio data is sent to AssemblyAI, OpenAI, or Deepgram.
  • Translation: If you enable the translation feature, transcribed text is sent to the Google Translate API.

3. Data Security

We implement security best practices by:

  • Using secure HTTPS connections for all data sent to AI providers.
  • Ensuring all settings and API keys stay within your browser environment.
  • Requesting the minimum necessary permissions (storage) required for functionality.

4. User Control

You have full control over your data. You can remove your API keys or change your transcription preferences at any time through the Extension's Preferences page. Uninstalling the Extension will immediately remove all stored settings from your browser.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Chrome Web Store and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
